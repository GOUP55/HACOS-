🌅 HMC Logo Design Proposals

Pattern 1: Bold Geometric
Inspired by Billionaire Boy Club visual language — thick, impactful letterforms with internal decorative patterns.
Visual Strategy:
Bold, geometric sans-serif creates immediate impact. Internal pattern (sunrise diamonds) subtly references morning awakening. Ideal for bold signage and brand presence.
Pattern 2: Elegant Serif
Inspired by BBC (Billionaire Boys Club) refined approach — sophisticated serif typography with accent details.
Visual Strategy:
Elegant serif italic conveys sophistication and wellness authority. Minimalist accent elements (sunrise, dew) suggest natural recovery. Perfect for premium brand communications and packaging.

Logo Usage Recommendation

Pattern 1 (Bold Geometric) is recommended for:
• Large signage and outdoor applications
• Social media and digital marketing
• Merchandise and branded materials
• Bold, attention-grabbing contexts

Pattern 2 (Elegant Serif) is recommended for:
• Premium packaging and stationery
• Brand communications and editorials
• Sophisticated digital interfaces
• Events and formal communications

Design Principles Applied

Both patterns embody HMC's core brand values:
Sophistication: Refined typography that signals premium positioning
Wellness: Subtle natural motifs (sunrise, diamonds, minimal elements)
Clarity: Bold, legible forms that work across all media scales
Timelessness: Classic letterforms that won't feel dated
